PHYS109 - Introduction to Astrophysics

This course provides an introduction to the fundamental concepts of astrophysics, including the study of stars, galaxies, and the universe as a whole.

Course Information

  • Course Page: UiB Course Page
  • Type of Exam: Written exam
  • Course Activities:
    • Lectures
    • Seminars
    • Lab work
  • Recommended Literature:
    • "Introduction to Astrophysics" by XYZ
    • "Astrophysics for Beginners" by ABC

Course Goals and Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the basic principles of astrophysics.
  • Learn about the lifecycle of stars.
  • Study the structure and dynamics of galaxies.
  • Explore cosmology and the evolution of the universe.
